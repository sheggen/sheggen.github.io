Means and Frequencies

Watch Quick Guide

Means and frequencies tables included this portfolio's maximum detail for your survey results, including peer-by-peer columns, percentages, counts, and, for Likert-scale items, means and standard deviations. These tables provide an accessible alternative to the visualizations elsewhere in your CAO report. Each table may be exported to CSV / Excel format.

See the Means subsection for means and standard deviations.

See the Frequencies subsection for counts, percentages, and responses not conforming to a five-point Likert metric(e.g., "I don't know" responses).
