Benchmarks

Most of the questions in the COACHE Faculty Job Satisfaction Survey are five-point Likert scale items. COACHE Benchmarks are the unweighted arithmetic means of several items that fall within the same theme. Benchmarks allow the reader to understand how faculty feel about a particular issue without reading each individual item. In these views, the reader gets a general perspective on the results and guides them to the sections that warrant a deeper dive.

Benchmarks at a Glance

These pages visualize your institution’s results compared to peers, the entire cohort, and your prior survey administration. You can filter the view by tenure status, rank, gender, and race.

Benchmarks Dashboard

This visualization emphasizes the different experiences of comparable groups at your institution (e.g., men compared to women). You can also view your results by disciplinary categories like humanities, social sciences, and so on.

Divisional Analysis

A link to this chart appears if your institution provided each faculty member’s school, college, or other academic division in your original population file.
