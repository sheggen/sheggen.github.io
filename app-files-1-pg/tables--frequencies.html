Frequencies

The links at the left lead to tables with counts and percentages for every item in your CAO Report. Data are reported overall, by rank, by tenure status, by gender, and by race/ethnicity. Unlike the data visualizations, these tables report your five peers' results individually. (The order of the peers' columns has been randomized.) They also include responses such as "I don't know" or "Not applicable," which are not calculated in your Benchmark ratings.

When viewing a table, click "Download table" to export it to CSV / Excel format so that you can run your own analysis or build your own custom displays.

We have filtered any results where your institution had fewer than five respondents. Therefore, you can share these tables with anyone at your institution and not be concerned about issues of confidentiality.
