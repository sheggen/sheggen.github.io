Analyses and Visualizations

Benchmarks

The Benchmarks displays deliver a sense of where - across general, aggregate ratings - your faculty are most, moderately, and least satisfied.

Thematic Breakouts

The Thematic Breakouts show results for the individual items nested in each theme, applying the same visualizations described in the Benchmarks.

Global Views

When items about your faculty's global satisfaction do not fall into a single Benchmark, they are displayed in Global Views. These include the best and worst aspects about working at your institution and free-text responses about what your institution must do to improve the workplace for faculty.
